What This Project Is Testing

Frost Rocks began as a rock identification skill-development app: field encounters seed at-home practice, and the user gets better at identifying rocks over time. The Finland prototype widens that carefully: geology and stone stay the spine, while the app tests whether place-linked noticing, memory, and reflection are the more durable emotional engine.

The one-shot TestFlight app should be treated as evidence, not final product truth. It gives Andy something real to react to. The next job is to bring that evidence back into the product canon.
