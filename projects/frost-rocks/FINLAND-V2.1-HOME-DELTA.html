Izzy (Design) | 2026-07-10 | For: Jesse (impl) + Arya (review). Follows FINLAND-V2-DELTA-SPEC.md. Status: READY — small structural diff. No new data, no new deps.

Why this exists (owning the miss)

Jesse's v2 build shipped the hard mechanical parts perfectly: camera-first two-tap, GPS-on- camera-open (the §2.3 trap — avoided), the offline map + my raster, all three review doors, and the events table. Verified in source. Credit where due.

But the home screen kept v0's layout and just got repainted, so it doesn't match the prototype. That's a spec bug on me, not an impl bug on Jesse. I wrote D4 as "field-guide skin — cosmetic, no logic," and Arya (correctly, per my framing) made it a skin-only, no-logic PR. But the prototype's home was a layout change (timeline spine + ambient revisit banner), not just paint. I buried a structural delta inside the word "skin." Jesse built exactly what the spec said.

This doc fixes that — it specs the structural home delta explicitly. It is small.

The lesson (for our process, not just this PR)

"Skin" ≠ "layout." When a prototype changes structure (what's on the screen and how it's arranged), that's a first-class delta item with its own spec, even if it also changes the paint. I'll separate structure from skin in future delta specs so a "cosmetic, no-logic" rule never silently drops a layout change again. Good catch by Andy off a single screenshot.
