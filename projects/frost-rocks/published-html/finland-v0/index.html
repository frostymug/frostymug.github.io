What evidence would make this more certain?

Draft learning note

Possible context, not a final answer

Banding can point toward gneiss, but a photo cannot confirm the full mineral makeup. In Finland, ancient bedrock and glacially exposed surfaces make this a useful place to practice the granite/gneiss distinction.
